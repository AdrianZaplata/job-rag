## Responsibilities
- Design, train, and deploy ranking and recommendation models (learning-to-rank, collaborative filtering, two-tower systems)
- Build and optimize NLP models for text classification, information extraction, and embeddings using BERT variants
- Integrate modern ML frameworks (Hugging Face Transformers, LangChain)
- Work with large datasets and optimize for effectiveness and scalability
- Monitor production model performance

- NLP knowledge including transformer architectures
- Participation in recommendation systems and ranking projects
- Proficiency in JavaScript or TypeScript for ML solutions
- Analytical thinking, ability to evaluate model impact on business goals
- Experiment tracking and monitoring tools experience
- Cloud platform familiarity (GCP, AWS, Azure) and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Strong communication skills for explaining technical concepts to non-technical audiences
