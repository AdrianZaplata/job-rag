These three are grouped because they have NO inter-dependency (the wiring between them happens at composition time in `envs/prod/main.tf`) and they produce outputs that the remaining three modules (database, compute, identity) consume. Picking up here means Plan 04 (database + compute + identity modules) can run in parallel in the same wave once Plan 03 lands its outputs (or they can both be sequenced via files_modified disjoint sets — they are disjoint).

- **network** = `azurerm_container_app_environment` (raw azurerm per D-03; AVM module is pre-stable). One resource, takes a LAW workspace ID as input.

- **monitoring** = AVM LAW `0.5.1` + diagnostic settings + budget. The diagnostic setting takes `aca_id` as a variable; the consumer (envs/prod/main.tf) wires it AFTER the compute module produces `aca_id`.

Output: 12 files (3 modules × 4 files). All modules pass static validation. Plan 04's three modules consume outputs declared here.

  time_grain      = "Monthly"
